About Gondilal Kiva Gold & Diamond Jewellery

Welcome to Gondilal Kiva Gold & Diamond Jewellery

Gondilal Kiva is a leading jeweller in Banda, Uttar Pradesh. We offer diamond jewellery, gold jewellery and silver jewellery for daily wear and special occasions. The latest showroom from the Gondilal Saraf family which is well known for its jewellery in Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh for the last 100 years. While the name Gondilal promises tradition and trust, Kiva promises beauty and novelty in its designs, thus Gondilal-Kiva provides much-needed freshness without breaking away from the culture which we have grown with over the years.
